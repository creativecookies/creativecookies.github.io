MLSE Foundation

Email, Ecommerce

The MLSE Foundation has been able to reinvent their 50|50 by making it an online event in the past year. This solution is based on selling tickets and giving users a chance for that in-person experience of being at the Raptor's game from home.

The MLSE had a need for an ecommerce solution that would increase sales for their online 50|50. I designed and coded this site with the Shopify platform. The main goal of the site was to lets users know how high the jackpot was! Creating an intuitive user experience was my goal - taking care of everything from design through development made it happen seamlessly.
