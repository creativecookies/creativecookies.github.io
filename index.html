About

We met in the summer of 2021 working at a digital ad agency. Working side by side, as copywriter and creative director, we soon became the dream team at our agency, building websites and launching influential advertising campaigns.

Our secret ingredient: chemistry. Bonding over wretched diet culture, brands we love and planning the launch of a podcast (that never seems to launch).

We’ve since parted ways but have dreamed of getting the band back together. Until now. The dream team is back and we’ve got cookies.

Together, we have over 7 years of web and copywriting experience. Our cookie philosophy is easy. Get to know you and your brand and create a cookie people won’t be able to resist. We’ll work with you every step of the way to design, write and create a website for your unique brand.
